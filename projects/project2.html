View project

Description


An E-commerce application for buying cool technical gadgets


This e – commerce application is developed in order to get a strong hold on the MERN stack . It’s amazing how new tech gadgets and products have a way of solving everyday problems and simplifying tasks in our lives. We love discovering these innovative, new gadgets and why didn’t I think of that to help with all of our daily to-do’s . Here we gathered the cool tech gadgets we can’t live without.
The user begins by searching for a product, then adding his favourite products to cart , then continue to buy those products . The products listed out can be added to cart and favourites separately. Premium version will give access to zero delivery charges and early access to special sales, with some additional discounts.
The overall approach is to begin with the Front end of the website by using React modules and then continue efficiently to the backend and database part. Mongo DB will be used to implement database which stores user id and their respective information and products.
